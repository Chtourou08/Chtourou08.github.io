My experience with Cryptocurrency’s website spam

Nowadays, everyone is dreaming to be rich in the fastest way. That’s why many people are attracted to trading because they think it’s so easy to make millions by just clicking some buttons. And one of these persons is me sadly!
Today, there is a phone number that started calling me many times.

I didn’t answer because I was busy working on a project so I just googled the number to see who’s this strange but guess what, I found out these two links:

By curiosity, I decided to track this website and to check the content and it took me to a website called “ Miner Coin Exchange”. I felt confused because why someone is letting his email address and password on a link like that, the only explanation is that someone wants to cheat people and spam them or steal money/information from them. So I took a decision to check everything related to this website using buildwith as a tool to check his structure and sitegarding to see the spams exist.

I wrote this article to send a message to all people interested in Cryptocurrency and Easy-money, please be careful because hackers are waiting for your curiosity to drive you to his games. I also want to ask anyone who experiment something like this to share it with the public on forums or blogs so we can protect each other.

Also, I want to ask a question, why google is showing such links like that with passwords and spammed links? Is google safe for us or maybe google himself wants us to fall to these tricks?

Thank you for reading this short story and stay safe ;)
