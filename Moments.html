Moments

Do you think that everything is amiss in your life, guess what? It’s more than that, you don’t enjoy the moments as you should do because you don’t what you like exactly. Sometimes you feel like you need someone to be with you but sometimes you prefer to stay alone without talking to anyone.

What should you understand yourself? Who knows, it’s just a matter of time and you will discover that nothing stays as before. Maybe that’s the problem, you’re looking for new experiences all the time but you suffer from all pain when everything is changing! Maybe it depends on if the resources you have and how much money you can spend or maybe you have more than one personality and every time you need to satisfy one of them. But do you think you can keep going like that?

You cannot put 1 liter of water in a cup that can contain just half liter! I know it’s it that great example but you understand what I mean. So let’s summarize what we wrote, life is about changing so all the time people will change and environments also (otherwise it’s gonna be boring).

So what you should think about is how can you adapt yourself so when everything is the same you can live with that and continue what you’re doing without feeling sadness or nostalgia, otherwise if everything starts changing you should accept that and update the idea you have in your mind and the way you’re thinking.

If there is no rain, plants will die and if there is just winter, we will not have any fruits so all the seasons are necessary maybe not now but it will be later one day so just lives the moment as it is and be ready for the next moment…

I hope that all the ideas that I talked about are clear for you and I wish that you will enjoy your life as much as you can in every minute in this life. (Motivation vibes :p)
